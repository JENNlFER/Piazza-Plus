As a platform, Piazza has excellent affordances but it can be difficult to use. Too much information is presented at once leading to information overload, and interactive elements are difficult to find and in unintuitive places. These and many more problems make it extremely difficult to navigate piazza in real time when used in class. Students are unable to keep up with the instructor and face-to-face instructional quality can be dimished.
